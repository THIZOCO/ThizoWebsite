Privacy Policy

Thizo, Inc. (“Thizo”) is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who interact with our websites and services. We provide this privacy policy to inform you about our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of personal information we receive when you interact with us.

Collection of Information

Thizo collects information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, request information, subscribe to a service, participate in interactive features, or otherwise communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other information you choose to provide.

Thizo also automatically collects certain information through your use of our services, including log files, cookies, and web beacons. This information may include your IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed, and other user-specific details.

Use of Information

We may use the information we collect from you to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.

Additionally, we may aggregate or de-identify your information, so it no longer identifies you personally. We may use this information for any lawful purpose, including research and analysis.

Sharing of Information

Thizo may share the information we collect from you in the following ways:

  • With your consent or at your direction.
  • With vendors and other third-party service providers who need access to your information to carry out work on our behalf.
  • In response to a request for information if we believe disclosure is required or permitted by law.
  • If we believe your actions are inconsistent with our terms or policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of Thizo or others.
